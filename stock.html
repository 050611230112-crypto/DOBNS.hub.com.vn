Investor Criteria

Items Contents Note
Diversify portfolio The selected stocks must belong to a variety of different sectors It helps reduce unsystematic risks and avoid dependening on a single industry
Growth The company has stable growth (revenue, EPS) This aligns with a profit target of around 20% and ensures accumulation for long-term goals (raising children and purchasing assets)
Financial efficiency ROE and ROA are at high and stable levels Ensure that businesses use capital efficiently and have a solid financial foundation
Enterprise capitalization Large or medium-sized capitalization companies Reduced risk compared to small-cap stocks, suitable for investors with families and financial obligations
